About RediGoo

RediGoo is India's emerging and innovative online bus ticketing platform, dedicated to revolutionizing the way people plan and book their road travel. Built with reliability, ease of use, and accessibility in mind, RediGoo offers seamless bus ticket booking options across cities, states, and regions.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a group of transport and tech professionals, RediGoo was born out of the need for a more transparent, user-friendly, and secure way to travel by bus. Initially starting in South India with a small network of bus operators, we quickly expanded due to our commitment to customer satisfaction, operator partnerships, and digital innovation.

Today, RediGoo connects thousands of routes and has become a trusted travel partner for daily commuters, students, professionals, and leisure travelers.

Our Mission

To provide a smarter, safer, and more enjoyable bus travel experience through reliable technology, real-time booking, and excellent customer support — ensuring that every journey is stress-free and comfortable.

What We Offer

✓ Easy-to-use web platform and mobile experience
✓ Real-time seat availability and booking confirmation
✓ Safe and secure payment options
✓ Partnered with top-rated bus operators nationwide
✓ 24/7 customer support

Looking Ahead

As we grow, we continue to invest in smarter technologies, expand our route network, and improve user experience — so your journey with RediGoo always stays ahead of the curve.
